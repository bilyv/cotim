## Getting Started

The Coti Project Progress Tracker helps you organize and track your projects through a structured step-by-step approach. Whether you're managing personal goals, work projects, or learning objectives, this tool provides an intuitive interface for breaking down complex tasks into manageable steps.

### Accessing the Application

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to the application URL
3. You'll be presented with the sign-in page

## Creating an Account

To create an account:

1. Click the "Sign up" link on the sign-in page
2. Enter your full name
3. Provide your email address
4. Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Click "Create Account"
6. You'll be redirected to the dashboard

### Account Benefits

- Save your projects and progress
- Access your data from any device
- Track your activity over time
- Customize your experience

## Signing In

### With Existing Account

1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click "Sign In"

### Anonymous Access

For quick access without creating an account:
1. Click "Continue as Guest"
2. Start using the application immediately
3. Note: Your data will not be saved between sessions

## Managing Projects

### Creating a New Project

1. Click the "New Project" button on the dashboard
2. Enter a project name
3. Add an optional description
4. Select a color for the project
5. Click "Create Project"

### Viewing Projects

All your projects are displayed on the dashboard in a grid layout. Each project card shows:
- Project name
- Brief description (if provided)
- Progress indicator
- Completion percentage

### Editing Projects

To edit a project:
1. Click on the project card to open the project details
2. Make changes to the project information
3. Save your changes

### Deleting Projects

To delete a project:
1. Hover over the project card
2. Click the delete icon that appears
3. Confirm the deletion in the dialog box
4. Note: This will permanently delete the project and all its steps

## Working with Steps

### Adding Steps

1. Open a project by clicking on its card
2. Click "Add Step"
3. Enter a step title
4. Add an optional description
5. Click "Add Step"

### Completing Steps

1. Find the step you want to complete
2. Click the "Complete" button next to the step
3. The step will be marked as completed and crossed out

### Undoing Step Completion

1. Find the completed step
2. Click the "Undo" button next to the step
3. The step will be marked as incomplete

### Deleting Steps

1. Click the delete icon next to any step
2. Confirm the deletion in the dialog box

### Step Order

Steps are displayed in the order they were created. The first step is automatically unlocked, and subsequent steps become unlocked as you complete previous ones.

## Tracking Progress

### Progress Visualization

Each project includes multiple ways to track your progress:

1. **Progress Circle**: A visual indicator showing overall completion percentage
2. **Step Counter**: Shows how many steps you've completed out of the total
3. **Step Status**: Each step is color-coded based on its status:
   - Gray: Not yet unlocked
   - Blue: Unlocked but incomplete
   - Green: Completed

### Activity Tracking

The profile page includes:
- A contribution graph showing your activity over the past year
- Statistics on total projects and steps
- Overall completion rates

## Profile Management

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Click the profile icon in the top right corner
2. Select "View Profile" from the dropdown menu
3. Your profile information will be displayed

### Profile Information

Your profile shows:
- Your name and email
- Account creation date
- Activity statistics
- Contribution graph (similar to GitHub's)

### Activity Graph

The activity graph shows your daily contributions over the past year:
- Lighter colors indicate fewer activities
- Darker colors indicate more activities
- Hover over any day to see the exact count

## Theme Settings

### Switching Themes

1. Click the theme toggle icon in the top right corner
2. If currently in light mode, clicking will switch to dark mode
3. If currently in dark mode, clicking will switch to light mode

### Theme Persistence

- Your theme preference is saved automatically
- The application will remember your choice between sessions
- The default theme respects your system preferences

### Benefits of Each Theme

**Light Mode**:
- Clean, bright interface
- Good for well-lit environments
- Classic appearance

**Dark Mode**:
- Reduces eye strain in low light
- Modern appearance
- Can help conserve battery on OLED screens

## Tips and Best Practices

### Organizing Projects

1. Create separate projects for different areas of your life (work, personal, learning)
2. Use descriptive names and colors to quickly identify projects
3. Break large goals into smaller, manageable steps

### Using Steps Effectively

1. Make step titles specific and actionable
2. Use descriptions to add context or detailed instructions
3. Complete steps in order to maintain momentum
4. Don't skip steps - each one builds on the previous

### Tracking Progress

1. Regularly review your projects to stay on track
2. Celebrate completing projects to maintain motivation
3. Use the activity graph to establish consistent habits
